# Common Patterns Observed Across All 10 Experts

## Pattern 1: Every Expert Has a Named System
None of the 10 experts just "post content."
Every single one has a documented, named framework whether Edutainment, 95-5, Zero Click, 3S Strategy, or Hook Bank.
The framework is their brand.

## Pattern 2: Contrarian Hooks Dominate
The highest-performing posts across all 10 experts open with a statement that challenges conventional wisdom.
"Lead generation is dead." "Most B2B content is useless."

## Pattern 3: Personal Story + Business Lesson
Every expert combines a personal moment or real experience
with a business takeaway. Pure theory or educational content gets low engagement.
Pure personal gets low business value.
Personal story during business learning is what works the best.

## Pattern 4: Zero-Click Value Delivery
Across all 10 experts, posts that deliver the full insight inside LinkedIn itself outperform posts that drive traffic elsewhere. Amanda Natividad's Zero Click philosophy shows up in everyone's best work.

## Pattern 5: Consistency Over Volume
None of the 10 expert post every day. Most post 3-5 times per week.
The pattern is being consistent, not exhausting.

## Pattern 6: Community and Conversation
Dave Gerhardt and Chris Walker both built communities (Exit Five, Refine Labs podcast) that amplify their LinkedIn reach. The best LinkedIn content drives
conversation in other channels too and help build community.
